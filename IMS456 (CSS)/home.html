PERTUBUHAN KEBAJIKAN ANAK-ANAK YATIM JOHOR


Bismillahirrahmanirrahim.

Assalamualaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh,

"Help the orphans, contact them now!.. The blessings and rewards of helping orphans are immeasurable, donate as sincerely as possible."

IN the Quran 23 times the word 'orphan' is mentioned and the use of the word orphan refers to poverty and fatherlessness. It means that those who are in the orphan group (orphans) need attention and defense from society to enable them to live comfortably and perfectly like other people who have a guardian (father or mother). Al-Quran clearly affirms in some of its verses that tells us to do good to orphans. Surah al Baqarah, verse 220, Allah SWT said meaning; "They ask you about orphans, say: Improving their condition is good." It was narrated from Abu Ummah that the Prophet SAW said which means: "Whoever caresses the head of an orphan for the sake of Allah SWT, he will receive a lot of good from every hair that is caressed. And whoever does good to orphans, girls and boys, then he and I will be in heaven like this, the Prophet SAW gestured stretching between his index and middle fingers." (Hadith narrated by Ahmad) Commenting on the hadith of Ahmad's history, Al-Hafizah Ibnu Hajar Al-Asqalani said: "It is enough as proof that the position of the Prophet is close to the position of a person who takes care of an orphan, the fact that there is no other finger between the middle finger and the index finger."

Wassalamualaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh.
