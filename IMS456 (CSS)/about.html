HISTORY

On a 4 acre plot of land in Johor Bahru's Jalan Bukut Cagar, the Johor Orphan Welfare Organization (PKAYJ) was constructed. The Johor Islamic Religious Council has established a waqf on the construction site. Originally a board building, this office complex has two pillars beneath which a vault contains some of the staff. The Rumah Barkat office underwent an RM 2 million renovation in 2012, using ready-made stone. The cost of constructing this house was covered by public donations. The Johor Orphans' Welfare Organization is in charge of this house, which is situated below (PKAYJ). This organisation was started in with the intention of protecting orphans all over the state.

The building of Rumah Barkat is another effort to aid orphans who are enrolled in school and is in keeping with the organization's founding mission to protect orphans and other vulnerable people and to mentor them in achieving academic success and upholding moral principles.

VISSION

"Making Rumah Barkat, the Johor Orphans Welfare Organization as a Home The Ultimate Dream of Orphans”

MISSION

"Giving awareness to orphans that orphans are not a wall obstacles to achieving outstanding life goals”
